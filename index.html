Charitable giving

For churches

The average giving by adults who attend US Protestant churches is about $17 a week.[18][19]

Demographics

Millennials

84% of Millennials give to charity, donating an annual average of $481 across 3.3 organizations.[18]

Baby Boomers

72% of Boomers give to charity, donating an annual average of $1,212 across 4.5 organizations.[18]

Greatest Gen

88% of the Greatest gen gives to charity, donating an annual average of $1,367 across 6.2 organizations.[18]
